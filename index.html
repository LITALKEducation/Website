No prior level is required. We accept complete beginners through to advanced speakers. You will complete a short placement test before your first lesson so we can match you with the right teacher and program.

LITALK offers both online and in-person lessons. Online sessions are conducted via Zoom or Google Meet. In-person sessions are available at our Bangkok location. You can mix and match based on your schedule.

Most students notice a significant improvement in confidence within 4–6 weeks. Measurable fluency improvements typically come after 3–4 months of consistent learning. Results depend on your starting level, frequency of lessons, and practice outside class.

You can cancel or reschedule any lesson with at least 24 hours notice at no charge. Cancellations within 24 hours are counted as a used lesson. We also offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all new students.
