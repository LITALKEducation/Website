Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how LITALK Education collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal data when you use our website, programs, and Student Portal, in accordance with Thailand's Personal Data Protection Act.

Effective Date: July 8, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how LITALK Education ("LITALK", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal data when you visit our website, enrol in our programs, or use the Student Portal. We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (2019) of Thailand ("PDPA") and other applicable data protection laws.

2. Data Controller

For the purposes of the PDPA, LITALK Education is the data controller responsible for the personal data collected through our Services. You can reach us using the contact details in Section 13 below.

3. Information We Collect

  • Identity & contact data: name, date of birth, email address, phone number, and, where applicable, a parent or guardian's contact details for minor students.
  • Account & learning data: Student Portal login credentials, program enrolment, lesson schedules, attendance, progress reports, placement test results, and instructor feedback.
  • Payment data: billing details and transaction records processed via our payment partners; we do not store full payment card numbers on our own servers.
  • Communication data: messages sent through the Student Portal, contact forms, email, or our official LINE account.
  • AI chat data: messages you send to our AI chat assistant (Nong Lilly) in the Student Portal, together with the limited account snapshot (such as class schedule, credit balance, and payment status) used to generate a response, and the resulting conversation history.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data collected via cookies and similar technologies (see Section 6).

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Student Portal account and deliver our English learning programs.
  • To schedule, conduct, and record (where applicable) lessons and track your learning progress.
  • To process payments and issue receipts or invoices.
  • To communicate with you about your account, lessons, and updates to our Services.
  • To respond to enquiries and provide customer support.
  • To operate the AI chat assistant (Nong Lilly) and generate responses to your questions about your schedule, credits, and payments; and to review chat conversations to develop and improve the assistant and our Services.
  • To improve our Services, curriculum, and website through analytics.
  • To send marketing communications, where you have provided consent, which you may withdraw at any time.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal bases under the PDPA: (a) your consent; (b) performance of a contract with you, such as enrolling you in a program; (c) our legitimate interests, such as improving our Services and preventing fraud, provided these interests do not override your fundamental rights; and (d) compliance with a legal obligation.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate our website, remember your language preference, and understand how visitors use our Services. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website.

7. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your personal data with:

  • Instructors and staff, to the extent necessary to deliver your lessons.
  • Service providers who support our operations, such as payment processors, cloud hosting providers, video conferencing platforms, and communication tools (e.g. LINE), under contractual obligations to protect your data.
  • Google, via the Google Gemini API (Google Cloud), to process the messages and limited account snapshot needed to generate a response when you use the AI chat assistant (Nong Lilly). We only send the information required to answer your specific question — never your full account or other students' data.
  • Legal and regulatory authorities, where required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.
  • A successor entity, in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to equivalent data protection commitments.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our Services, comply with legal, tax, and accounting requirements, and resolve disputes. Student records and payment records are generally retained for the duration of your enrolment plus a further period as required by Thai law. When your data is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymise it.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures — such as access controls, encryption in transit, and restricted staff access — to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Rights Under the PDPA

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access and obtain a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Request deletion or anonymisation of your personal data, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your personal data.
  • Request data portability, where technically feasible.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Personal Data Protection Committee (PDPC) of Thailand if you believe your rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 13.

11. Children's Privacy

Our programs are open to students of various ages, including minors. Where a student is a minor, we require the consent of a parent or legal guardian to process the student's personal data, and we collect only the information reasonably necessary to provide our educational Services to that student.

12. International Data Transfers

Some of our service providers (such as cloud hosting providers, video conferencing platforms, and the Google Gemini API (Google Cloud) for the AI chat assistant) may process data outside Thailand. Where we transfer personal data internationally, we take steps to ensure the receiving party provides an adequate level of data protection, as required by the PDPA.

13. Contact Us / Data Protection Officer

If you have questions, requests, or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal data, please contact us at privacy@litalkeducation.com, through our Contact page, or via our official LINE account. You may also contact the Office of the Personal Data Protection Committee (PDPC), Thailand, if you are not satisfied with our response.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
